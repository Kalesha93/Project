Welcome to SK Dance Academy

Introduction

Dance Academy is where passion meets performance. Whether you’re 5 or 50, a beginner or a pro, we’ve got a space for you to move, learn, and grow.

At SK, we believe dance is more than movement — it’s expression, discipline, and joy. Founded in 2015 in Tiruchanur, our academy has trained over 500 students in 10+ dance forms, from timeless Bharatanatyam to energetic Hip-Hop. With certified choreographers and a passion for excellence, we help every student find their rhythm, build confidence, and shine on stage.
