WELCOME TO SK Academy

Tirupati's Leading Dance Academy Since 2015

At SK, we believe dance is for everyone. From 5-year-olds taking their first steps to adults rediscovering their passion.

Founded with a vision to make professional dance training accessible, we’ve trained 500+ students in 12+ dance forms. Our students have performed on national TV, won inter-state competitions, and most importantly - gained confidence that lasts a lifetime.

OUR PURPOSE

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide world-class dance education that nurtures creativity, discipline, and confidence in every student. We are committed to making professional training accessible to all age groups through expert faculty, personalized attention, and performance opportunities that transform passion into artistry.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Rayachoti's most trusted dance academy that empowers individuals through movement. We envision creating a community where students not only learn dance but discover self-expression, cultural roots, and lifelong confidence that extends beyond the studio to every stage of life.

OUR JOURNEY

10 Years of Dancing Dreams

What started as a small evening batch in a rented hall has grown into Rayachoti's most loved dance academy. Here's how we got here.

2015 - The Beginning

Started with 12 students and 2 dance forms: Bharatanatyam & Bollywood. First studio in Tiruchanur.

2018 - First Milestone

100+ students enrolled. Launched Hip-Hop and Contemporary batches. First annual show with 200+ audience.

2020 - Going Digital

Started online classes during lockdown. Trained students from 5 states. YouTube channel crossed 10K subscribers.

2023 - New Studio

Moved to 2000 sq.ft AC studio with wooden flooring. Added Zumba, Kathak, and Kids special batches.

2026 - Today

500+ active students, 12+ dance styles, 8 expert faculty. Govt recognized certification courses launched.
